NEBULA - Xplorer

Welcome to the NEBULA-Xplorer project
a pioneering Dutch satellite mission exploring how black holes consume material from nearby stars. Launching around 2030, it aims to unlock new insights into black holes and the origins of the universe. Here, you can learn more about this exciting mission and how it connects science, education, and innovation.
